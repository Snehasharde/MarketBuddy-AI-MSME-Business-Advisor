Key Steps in Portfolio Construction

Constructing a strong portfolio involves a structured approach:

  • 1. Identify Goals: Define short, medium, and long-term objectives like buying a house or retirement.
  • 2. Assess Risk Tolerance: Understand how much risk you can handle based on income and life stage.
  • 3. Asset Allocation: Divide investments among equity, debt, gold, real estate, and other assets.
  • 4. Diversification: Spread investments to reduce risk and improve stability.
  • 5. Periodic Review: Rebalance your portfolio regularly to stay aligned with goals.
